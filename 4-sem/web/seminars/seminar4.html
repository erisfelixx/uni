Detailed info
Foxes are small, clever animals that belong to the Canidae family, which also includes dogs, wolves, and coyotes. They are known for their bushy tails and pointed ears. Foxes can be found in many parts of the world, including forests, grasslands, and even cities.

About ducks
Main features
Detailed info
